Sector Zero

The Central Core

The fortified heart of Tenseishi. A 60km-diameter circular district where power, wealth, and authority converge.

Overview

Sector Zero sits at the geographic and political center of Tenseishi. Encircled by its own fortified perimeter, accessible only through three massive gates spaced at equal intervals, the sector is both a symbol of authority and a practical fortress within the greater city.

The Kage's compound crowns an elevated hill at the very center — a deliberate statement of power visible from every corner of the district. The sector is organized in concentric rings radiating outward from this central hill, each ring serving a distinct function.

Layout — The Three Rings

Inner Ring — The Government Core

Surrounding the Kage's hilltop compound, the inner ring houses the institutions that run the city. Military command headquarters where Uchiha Akashi oversees all sector operations. The ANBU-equivalent's base of operations. The Council of Elders' chambers. Research facilities and archives — including classified installations known only to the Kage, Akashi, Kal Toven, and select council members.

Middle Ring — Elite Residential

The residential band where Tenseishi's most powerful families maintain their estates. Wealthy compounds, private gardens, and clan properties line wide, well-maintained avenues. Civilian staff live in quarters attached to the elite compounds they serve — a feudal arrangement where their residence in Sector Zero is tied directly to their employer.

Outer Ring — Industrial & Commercial

The outermost ring sits nearest to the three gates, handling the sector's logistics and industry. Factories of various types — oil processing, furniture manufacturing, and more — operate here, many under the Toven Inc umbrella. Stadiums, commercial facilities, and transport infrastructure service the sector's needs. This ring is what visitors see first when entering through the gates.

Key Locations

The Kage's Compound

Elevated on a hill at the sector's exact center. The seat of supreme authority, overseeing all of Tenseishi.

Military Command HQ

Inner ring. Where Uchiha Akashi coordinates all sector commanders and military operations across the city.

Council of Elders' Chambers

Inner ring. Meeting hall for the influential elders who advise the Kage, including Kal Toven.

Toven Inc Headquarters

Spread across all three rings — corporate offices in the inner ring, factories in the outer ring, and a private estate in the residential ring.

The Three Gates

Massive fortified entry points spaced equally around the perimeter, connecting to the junction system that leads to the outer sectors.

Hidden Archives

Location classified. Contains forbidden jutsu records, cataclysm research, and experimental technology. Access restricted to a select few.

Governance

Tenseishi is governed by a layered hierarchy rooted in Sector Zero. The Kage holds supreme authority. Directly beneath him serves Uchiha Akashi — the Kage's right hand — who oversees all three sector commanders. The Council of Elders provides advisory influence, with Kal Toven as its most powerful member due to his economic reach through Toven Inc.

Sector Zero's civilian population consists primarily of household staff attached to elite compounds. There is no independent civilian district or labor organization — every resident's presence depends on their connection to a patron.

Access & Security

Entry to Sector Zero requires passage through one of the three gates, which are connected to the outer sectors via the junction and passage network. The 300m gap between Sector Zero's perimeter and the surrounding sectors creates a controlled buffer zone. Each ring functions as an additional security layer — entering the industrial ring is possible with clearance, but reaching the government core requires significant authorization.
