Tenseishi

A fortified metropolis spanning 100–120km in radius. Humanity's last bastion in a world of irradiated wasteland.

The Four Sectors

Sector Zero

The Central Core

A 60km-diameter fortified circle housing the Kage's compound, military command, the Council of Elders, and Toven Inc. Organized in concentric rings around a central hill. Accessible only through three massive gates.
Sector I

The Eastern Quarter

The affluent district. Flat terrain with wide boulevards and garden estates. Home to the Senju and Uzumaki clans. Features elite training grounds, a high-end commercial district, and pristine Konoha-style architecture.
Sector II

The Northern Quarter

The balanced sector. A dense urban core centered on the twins' command hall and central bazaar, with residential neighborhoods spreading outward. Home to both the Hyūga and Uchiha clans.
Sector III

The Western Quarter

The most populated and economically disadvantaged sector. Built against the cliff walls of an ancient gorge, featuring three vertical layers: Cliffside Row, the Mid-Warrens, and the shadowy Deep Warrens below.

The Wasteland Beyond

Beyond the great walls lies the Badlands — endless post-nuclear devastation stretching to the horizon. Dotted with 20–25 desperate settlements connected by dangerous trade routes, the wasteland is a constant reminder of why Tenseishi's walls must hold.

Trade caravans brave irradiated terrain, mutated wildlife, and bandit raids to bring resources the city cannot produce within its walls. Every settlement clings to existence, dependent on Tenseishi as much as the city depends on them.
